Moonglum of Mordor is a Black Númenórean [Retired].
This mighty Númenórean travels Arda cloaked in the darkness of Ungoliant,
carrying the Staff of Melkor, which, in its ancient form as a blackened spear,
smote dead the vile Trees of Valinor. Moonglum's mission is quite simple, To
destroy any and all of Manwe's minions in the name of Morgoth.
