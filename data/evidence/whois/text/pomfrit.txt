Pomfrit Potatohead, Arch Priest of the Shire is a level
    forty-seven Fallohide Patriarch [Retired].
Last login 21 years, 9 months, and 11 days ago from [numerical
    address].

As the son of a hobbit gardner, Pomfrit spent many days listening to
Sam "the Uncie" Gamgee and his tales. With his brawny cousin Meerak he
explored the most of Arda, and having studied the arts of healing, he
now ranks as a hobbit of surprising wisdom, as well as a loyal aid to
the General. Pomfrit has fought many evil opponents, even trolls, but
at long last his little legs have tired from his adventures. Nowadays
he is rarely found outside his beloved Shire, where he spends most of
his time enjoying a good pipe of old Toby's best on a low bench...
