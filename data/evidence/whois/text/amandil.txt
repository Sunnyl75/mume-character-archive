Amandil the Last Lord of Andúnië is a Vala (Architect) [Retired].

Lord of Andúnië and father of Elendil. Dismissed by Sauron he sailed into the
West to plead for the misdeeds of the Númenóreans. Upon reaching the shores of
Valinor, Manwë appeared and listened but would not change his plans for war.
Instead, he gave the Man the choice to return to the mortal land or join the
Valar in their struggle against evil. Amandil chose the latter and was trusted
with Grond, seized from the hands of Morgoth, and given tools to shape new
lands.
