Shriek the Black Nazgûl of Númenór is a Black Númenórean
    [Retired].

     Once Men of the Haradrim, Easterlings and black Númenórean races
     Who were given the evil gift of rings of Power by Sauron, the Dark
     Lord.  These Nazgúl were his mightiest lieutenants during the Second
     and third age of the sun.  However, as thier bodies and souls were so
     fully corrupted by evil, they ceased to be human and became wraiths
     entirely, thralls and haunting spirits.
