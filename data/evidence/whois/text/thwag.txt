Thwag the Right Hand of Mandos is a level forty Tarkhnarb
    Necromancer [Retired].

    Having caught the fancy of the Vala Mandos, he now strives to
send whiteskins to the blessed halls. This has several downsides,
however, as often his master calls on him at awkward times, forcing
him to throw caution to the wind as bloodlust takes control of his
soul. The life of a berserker ends with a death, too often his own,
yet he's not ready to abandon this realm just yet and always comes
back to reap the harvest of whiteskins.
