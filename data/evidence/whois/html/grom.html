Grom Hellscream is a level thirty-four Tarkhnarb Battlemaster
    [Retired].

This former tunnel worker quickly developed his well-known lust for blood
and fought his way up through the Goblin ranks. It is told that against
the hammer of Grom none can stand, and he now holds the Chieftain titles
in the clans of the Warsong, the Shadowmoon and the Shattered Hand.

"Looks like meat's back on the menu, boys!"
