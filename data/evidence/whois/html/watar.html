Watár the Foolish Snaga is a level thirty-two Tarkhnarb Assassin
    [Retired].

    An outcast of the orkish society, perhaps because of his lack of
manners or sulky, shy person, he has taken to the dodgy path, living
off his cunning and skill in stealth rather than his nearly nonexistant
wits. He has turned his lack of friends into an advantage, turning his
skills into a trade where close connections would be a liability instead
of an asset. He cares for no-one and none can gain leverage on him
using an other person.
