Vishnak the Dark Caress is a level sixty-two Tarkhnarb Veteran
    [Retired].

He was sent to take Enso's place among the Uruk-Hai of the Misty Mountains by
Grolushivak Rockbottom, the by far most respected leader of the outposts in the
north. He is now beginning to grow into the role of his predecessor and will no
longer hesitate to violently and relentlessly unleash his fury upon those who
oppose him or disobey his orders. Fear will once again be our greatest weapon.
