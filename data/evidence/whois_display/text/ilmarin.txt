Ilmarin, tracker of ancient paths is an Elf [Retired].
Last login 19 years, 2 months, and 22 days ago.
Born in Grey Havens Ilmarin was trained to defend the land from all evil. He,
as any other elf, loved the nature and the animals and learned how to live in
coexistence with them. He hunted the game only if needed and used all parts
for all kinds of equipment and food. His progress in the art of scouting and
pathfinding was recognized by the queen of Lothlorien who wanted him in her
service. Taking part in the defence of the sacred forest against the roaming
orcs of Moria his hatred against Saurons minions grew. He still reports back
to the queen now and then but has been given a more free role to fight evil all
over Arda.
