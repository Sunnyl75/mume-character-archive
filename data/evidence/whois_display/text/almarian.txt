Almarian - Queen of Númenor, Beloved of Meneldur is a Maia
    (Shaper).

During the Second Age, when Númenor was in its greatest might and splendor,
Almarian was Queen to Tar-Meneldur. There, in Armenelos, did they sit upon
their golden thrones. Now, being the Third Age, Almarian resides within the
Halls of Doom, farthest west in all of Valinor, aiding Námo in the building
of Middle-Earth.
