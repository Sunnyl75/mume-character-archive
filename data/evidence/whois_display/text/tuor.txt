Tuor of Vinyamar is a Maia (Cartographer) [Retired].

  After the fall of Gondolin Tuor with Idril Celebrindal and their son
Earendil the Blessed went to the sea, where Tuor built a great ship and with
his wife set sail into sunset and the West. Tuor alone of mortal Men was
numbered among the elder race, and was joined with the Noldor, whom he loved.
  Now he dwells in Valinor, helping Valar to shape Arda and heal its wounds
inflicted by Morgoth.
