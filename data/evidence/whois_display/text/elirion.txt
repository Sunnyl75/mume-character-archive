Elirion Omafael, ranger of the north is a level thirty-four
    Dúnadan Battlemaster [Retired].
Last login 20 years ago from *.se.
This is one of the rangers defending the west against the creatures of darkness
that roam the wilderness. Elirion isn't very educated, nor very intelligent,
actually he is rather dull, but that makes his fanatic sense of duty towards
the people of the west even stronger. He is a rather solitary figure who spends
most of his time serving the good cause, which in his case means that he is
keeping track of orcish movement in the wilderness, and occasionally doing
battle with them. What others might think doesn't concern Elirion very much,
part because he doesn't meet to much other people, and part because his mind is
so set at what he is doing is right.
