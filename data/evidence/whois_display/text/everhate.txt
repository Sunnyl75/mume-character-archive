Everhate the Slaanesh Daemonette is a level seventy-two Tarkhnarb
    Battlemistress [Retired].
On a foul night when the moon was full, the powerful shamans Thanquol
and Maag brought forth evil magiks and summoned the creature Everhate to
aid the orc warlord Norsu in his extermination of the softskins of the West.
Resembling more a twisted parody of an elf maiden than an orc, Everhate is
a fearsome sight to behold.
