Odium is a level nine Black Númenórean Wanderer [Retired].
Main Entry...: odi·um
Pronunciation: 'O-dE-&m
Etymology....: Latin, hatred, from odisse to hate; akin to Old English atol
               terrible, Greek odyssasthai to be angry
Date.........: 1602

1 : the state or fact of being subjected to hatred and contempt as a result of
    a despicable act or blameworthy circumstance
2 : hatred and condemnation accompanied by loathing or contempt : DETESTATION
3 : disrepute or infamy attached to something : OPPROBRIUM
