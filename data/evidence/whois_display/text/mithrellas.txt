    Heroine [Retired].

Mithrellas, one of the companions of the maidens of Nimrodel, strays
in the wooded hills of Belfalas, harboured by Imrazôr the Numenorean,
whose union blesses the son Galador, first and noble Lord of Dol Amroth.
