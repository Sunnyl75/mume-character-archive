Baghtru B, Defender of the Outpost is a level forty-three Orkish
    Veteran [Retired].

Once upon a time a takhnarb patrol let by the mighty Gruumsh,
ventured deep into Zaugurz teritory. They killed some of the
Zaugurz outposts and raped their women. One of those raped by
Gruumsh brought a child into this cruel world and named it Baghtru.
Now, grown up a little he volunteered for a place in one of the
Zaugurz outposts, so he could revenge his mother and kill as many
takhnarbs as he can. Now raising fast in power, he becomes an even
greater and greater foe to the foul takhnarbs.
